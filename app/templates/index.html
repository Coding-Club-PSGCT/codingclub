Coding Club IDEATION

An interdisciplinary project hackathon

Phase 2 has ended

Phase 2 results will be announced by 2nd November

Overview

We are calling all creative minds, problem solvers, the geeks and coders at heart to re-imagine the future with us.

Theme

Think and build impactful projects in the areas of computer vision and machine learning, guided by application contexts, derived from real-world use and to help improve the way we work and live, and to create value for our society.

Suggestions

  • Manufacturing
    Computer vision coupled with sensors can work wonders for critical equipment.
  • Retail
    Find a way to track checkout theft, scan errors and fraud.
  • Financial services
    Find a way to resolve billing disputes.
  • Healthcare
    Find a way for monitoring patients and suggesting effective treatments.
  • Agriculture
    Find a way for mapping, analysing soil, counting livestock, evaluating crop yield and it's ripeness and more.
  • Surveillance
    Find a way to enable more security in public places and surveillance solutions that detect abnormal activities and inform managers to intervene.
  • Textile and Fashion
    Find a way for investigation and evaluation of fibres, yarns and textile quality.
  • Open innovation
    Convince us that your idea is here to create value for society.
Note : All your ideas have to be implemented using machine learning or computer vision.

Timeline

Phase 1
(Ends on September 22, 2021, Project Proposal submission ends on October 10, 2021)

Registrations and Idea submissions :
Form a team of 2 to 5 members and submit your innovative ideas and solutions as per the project proposal template. Registration will start by September 6, 2021.

Phase 2
(Starts from October 18, 2021)

Presentation :
Explain your ideas in the form of power-point presentation to the jury. Only shortlisted students will move to the next stage and will be assigned mentors.
Each team will be notified of their schedule for presentation after one or two days from the submission of the project proposal.

Phase 3
(1st Week of November, 2021)

Prototyping :
Selected students have to present their prototype.

Review

I) End of November, 2021
II) End of December,2021

Final Phase
(2nd week of January, 2022)

Grand Finale :
Compete in the grand finale and present your innovative ideas to win big. Cash prizes and certificates will be given for the top 3 projects. Participation certificates will be provided for all participants.

Rules

  • This is a team participation hackathon. The team size should be of 2 to 5 members (preferably members from different departments).
  • Each team can have members from the same department or from different departments.
  • You can take any of the suggested domains, but your projects should include machine learning or computer vision.
  • It is sufficient if one member in a team registers. Details of all the team members have to be submitted during registration. Project proposal can be submitted before October 10, 2021.
  • Your project proposal should be as per the guidelines mentioned in the template. Download the project proposal template .
  • Once the hackathon starts, you will get only one chance to submit your ideas.
  • Plagiarism will lead to disqualification of the team.
  • Failing to make the submissions on time will be liable to be rejected.
  • Your hack must be developed entirely during the Hackathon duration. You may use open source libraries and other freely available systems / services uch as Google Maps, Facebook Connect, Twitter feeds etc.
  • The intellectual property of your code belongs only to your team.

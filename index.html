Overview

We are calling all creative minds, problem solvers, the geeks and coders at heart to re-imagine the future with us.

Theme

Think and build impactful projects in the areas of computer vision and machine learning, guided by application contexts, derived from real-world use and to help improve the way we work and live, and to create value for our society.

Suggestions

  • Manufacturing
    Computer vision coupled with sensors can work wonders for critical equipment.
  • Retail
    Find a way to track checkout theft, scan errors and fraud.
  • Financial services
    Find a way to resolve billing disputes.
  • Healthcare
    Find a way for monitoring patients and suggesting effective treatments.
  • Agriculture
    Find a way for mapping, analysing soil, counting livestock, evaluating crop yield and it's ripeness and more.
  • Surveillance
    Find a way to enable more security in public places and surveillance solutions that detect abnormal activities and inform managers to intervene.
  • Textile and Fashion
    Find a way for investigation and evaluation of fibres, yarns and textile quality.
  • Open innovation
    Convince us that your idea is here to create value for society.
